Welcome to Beemember!

You are trying to help your bee friend to collect enough pollen for her hive. She does not know which flowers are poisonous, so it is up to you to remember for her! If you successfully lead her to the correct flowers, she will reward you with honey, but if you fail she will sting you!
Watch the flowers change colour and remember the order. Successfully land on 20 flowers to complete the game. You can survive 3 stings, after you have been stung 3 times you will lose the game.
