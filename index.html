Welcome to Beemember!

You are trying to help your bee friend to collect enough pollen for her hive. She does not know which flowers are poisonous, it is up to you to remember for her! If you successfully lead her to the correct flowers, she will reward you with honey, but if you fail she will sting you!
Watch the flowers appear and remember the order, once the bee sets off you must click on the flowers in the correct order to complete the game.
